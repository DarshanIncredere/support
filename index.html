Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I start a new game?
From the home screen, select your preferred difficulty level (Easy, Medium, or Hard) to start a new puzzle. You can also tap the refresh icon in the game screen to start a new game at the same difficulty.
Q: How do hints work?
Tap the "Hint" button during gameplay to reveal one correct number in a random empty cell. Use hints wisely as they are tracked in your statistics!
Q: How do I reset my statistics?
Go to the Profile screen by tapping the person icon, then tap the refresh/reset button in the top right corner. Confirm the reset when prompted. Note: This action cannot be undone.
Q: Why are some numbers shown in red?
Red numbers indicate incorrect entries that don't match the solution. This helps you identify and correct mistakes as you play.
Q: Does the app need internet connection?
No! Sudoku Edge works completely offline. An internet connection is only used to display optional advertisements.
Q: How is my best time calculated?
Your best time is recorded separately for each difficulty level when you successfully complete a puzzle. Only winning games count toward your best time.
Q: Can I change my player name?
Yes! Go to the Profile screen and tap the edit icon next to your name. Enter your new name and save.

ðŸ’¡ Pro Tips for Solving Sudoku

  • Start with easy cells: Look for rows, columns, or boxes with many filled numbers first.
  • Use elimination: If a number appears in a row and column, it can't be in the intersecting cell.
  • Check 3x3 boxes: Each box must contain numbers 1-9 without repetition.
  • Take your time: Rushing leads to mistakes. Think before placing a number.
  • Use highlights: Tap a cell to highlight its row and column for easier analysis.
